Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about learning with us

Do I need prior experience to start?

No! Our courses are designed for complete beginners. We start from the basics and build up gradually.

How much time do I need to dedicate?

We recommend 5-10 hours per week, but you can learn at your own pace. Consistency is more important than intensity.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium content. You can start with our free courses and upgrade when you're ready.

Can I get help if I'm stuck?

Yes! We have a supportive community and mentors available to help you through any challenges.
